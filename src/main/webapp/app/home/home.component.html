Journey supports you in tackling your personal challenges! Just follow these five steps.

  1. Start by defining your personal challenge. What is it you want to tackle? Provide a tag as short identifier and a description.
  2. For this challenge, come up with a hypothesis how to tackle it and define a concrete probe to test it. Make sure to add exit criteria when to stop and reflect.
  3. Now you're ready to go! With every step you make on your way towards your goal, create a journal entry to document your lessons and visualize your learning progress.
  4. While moving forward, keep an eye on your exit criteria.
  5. Finally: when the defined exit criteria are met, evaluate your probe and see if it could prove your hypothesis.

If you succeeded - hooray! You're ready to tackle your next challenge. :D

If you did not succeed yet, you at least learned what did not work in your specific context. Use that new knowledge to rinse and repeat and design a new hypothesis to tackle your challenge. All the best and keep going on your personal Journey!
